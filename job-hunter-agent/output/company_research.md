- **Scalability & Reliability:** Ensure services handle hundreds of thousands of requests per second with SLAs for latency (<100ms) and uptime (>99.99%).  
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Partner with Product Managers, Front-End Engineers, Data Scientists, and Site Reliability Engineers (SREs) across three continents.  
- **Technical Leadership:** Lead architecture discussions, define API contracts (REST/gRPC), and set technical direction for distributed systems.  
- **Observability & DevOps:** Implement monitoring (Prometheus, Grafana), logging (ELK stack), and alerting best practices; contribute to CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, Terraform).
